Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 21, 2026

MD Viewer ("we", "us", or "the site") is a free, browser-based Markdown viewer and editor available at mdview.co. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how it is used.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect personal information. MD Viewer does not require an account, login, or registration of any kind. We do not ask for your name, email address, or any other personally identifiable information.

2. Content You Create

All Markdown content you type or paste into the editor is processed entirely in your browser. Your content is never transmitted to our servers. We cannot see, access, or store anything you write.

3. Local Storage

We use your browser's localStorage to save your theme preference (dark or light mode). This data stays on your device and is never sent to any server. You can clear it at any time through your browser settings.

4. Cookies & Advertising

MD Viewer uses Google AdSense to display advertisements. Google may use cookies and similar technologies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this and other websites. Specifically:

  • Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads based on your browsing activity.
  • You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting Google Ads Settings.
  • You may also opt out of third-party vendor cookies by visiting aboutads.info.

We do not set any first-party tracking cookies ourselves.

5. Analytics

We may use privacy-respecting analytics to understand aggregate traffic patterns (e.g., page views, popular features). If analytics are enabled, no personally identifiable information is collected.

6. Third-Party Services

MD Viewer loads the following external resources to provide its features:

  • Google Fonts — to render typography (Inter, JetBrains Mono)
  • jsDelivr CDN — to load syntax highlighting language definitions
  • Google AdSense — to serve advertisements

Each of these services has its own privacy policy governing how they handle requests from your browser.

7. Children's Privacy

MD Viewer is a general-purpose tool and is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hello@mdview.co.
